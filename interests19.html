Research Interests (up to 2019)



Effective Field Theories of Topological Phases of Matter

  • Conformal field theories describing edge excitations in the fractional quantum Hall effect

  • Experimental signatures of fractional statistics in 2D

  • Stability of topological phases of matter in 2D and 3D

  • Surface excitations in 3D systems

  • Duality relations among field theories in (2+1) dimensions



Conformal invariance in two and higher dimensions

  • Numerical conformal bootstrap between two and four dimension

  • Irreversibility of the renormalization-group flow in more than two dimensions



History of physics

  • In 2012, together with F. Colomo, E. Castellani (Florence) and P. Di Vecchia (Stockholm) we edited and wrote a collective book on the early history of string theory
